## EmotivX 360 Viewer — Camera fix (mid-morning session, ~11:06 AM)

**Bug**: BallPathCamera was fighting OrbitControls in `useFrame` — camera lerp was overridden by OrbitControls' own updates every frame, resulting in camera drifting to ground level or black sky.

**Root cause**: Two systems writing `camera.position` and `controls.target` every frame simultaneously.

**Fix applied**:
1. Added `useEffect` that fires when `active` & `cameraLocked` switch on — snaps camera to near-overview position immediately so lerp starts from a sensible place
2. Added `camera.lookAt(overviewTarget)` in `useFrame` so camera always faces the pitch regardless of position drift
3. Disabled OrbitControls (`enabled={false}`, `enableDamping={false}`) when `showBallPath && cameraLocked` — prevents the two systems fighting
4. OrbitControls re-enabled when user clicks 🔓 Free Camera or exits ball path mode

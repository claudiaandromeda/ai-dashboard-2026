# Deem Tier Specification — Draft

| Tier | Price Range | Physical Product | Redeemable Value | Scarcity |
|------|------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------|
| Common | Free | Pen, sticker, small item | 20% off café/bar (single-use) | High volume |
| Uncommon | ~£4–5 | Branded item | £5 off club shop (min £25 spend) | Limited run |
| Rare | TBD | Quality garment | TBD | More limited |
| Epic | TBD | Premium garment | Exclusive experience (tunnel, meet & greet) | Very limited |
| Legendary | £150+ | Premium hoodie/garment | One-of-a-kind experience | 1-of-1 or tiny run |

## Hard Floor Principle
Every Deem is worth at least what you paid — in real, usable value. No speculation required.

## Redemption Mechanics
- **Common:** One-time QR barcode (Wetherspoons-style)
- **Uncommon:** Repeating discount, tracked against lifetime value cap
- **Rare:** Digital membership card, integrates with club systems
- **Epic:** Named experience voucher, redeemed via club events team
- **Legendary:** Concierge-managed, white-glove arrangement
